
## **1. Phase 1 Goals**

The goals of Phase 1 are to:

1. Display employee information in a prescribed format:

   * Employee Number
   * Employee Name
   * Birthday

2. Calculate the total number of hours worked in a week based on:

   * Daily log-in and log-out records
   * Official office hours
   * A 10-minute grace period for tardiness

3. Compute the **gross weekly salary** using a **monthly salary rate**.

4. Apply **government-mandated deductions** (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and withholding tax) on a **monthly basis**, converted proportionally to weekly deductions.

5. Display the **net weekly salary** in the console.

---

## **2. Assumptions and Constraints**

### **Assumptions**

* Employees are paid on a **monthly salary basis**.
* A month is assumed to have **4 weeks** for payroll computation.
* Attendance records are complete and accurately entered.
* Deduction rates are **simplified values** and do not reflect actual government rates.
* Office working days are **Monday to Saturday**.

### **Constraints**

* The system is **console-based only** (no graphical user interface).
* Data is entered manually via keyboard input.
* The system processes **one employee at a time**.
* No database or file storage is implemented in Phase 1.
* The system is developed using basic programming concepts suitable for **MO-IT101**.

---

## **3. Acceptance Criteria**

Phase 1 will be considered complete when:

* The system successfully accepts employee details via console input.
* The system correctly calculates weekly hours worked based on log-in and log-out times.
* The system computes the gross weekly salary from a monthly salary rate.
* Monthly deductions are correctly calculated and proportionally applied to weekly pay.
* The net weekly salary is accurately displayed.
* All outputs are clearly formatted and readable in the console.
* The program runs without errors and produces consistent results during testing.

---

## **5. Phase 1 Scope**

### **Included**

* Employee information input and display
* Attendance-based hour computation
* Weekly salary calculation
* Monthly deduction computation
* Net weekly salary calculation
* Console output formatting

### **Excluded**

* Inventory management
* Employee database integration
* User authentication
* Graphical user interface (GUI)
* Multi-employee processing
* Payslip generation or printing
